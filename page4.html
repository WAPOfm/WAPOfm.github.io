... a testimonial...

                    No matter where and when you look at, pollution is clearly observable in our environment. Be it on air, bodies of water, in our forests in land; pollution has become a serious threat to our normal every day life. Specifically, water pollution is a prominent problem nowadays. People of our generation seem to have lost the sense of discipline that is important in keeping our surroundings clean. Throw here, throw there, plastic bags here, wastes there: people keeps throwing their waste disposals onto our bodies of water. This kind of actions results to dead water, animals, and dead bodies of water.

                    WA-PO FM is created to help spread awareness about this very matter. As we can observe in our community, here in Bulacan, we can clearly see how serious water pollution is. The Meycauayan-Marilao river, named as one of the 30 dirties waterways in the whole world by a New York-based environmental group, is a good example of this problem. Through this campaign, our objective is to try and make people conscious of this case and convince them to make an action against it.

                    While building this campaign, we, the founder formulated many different ways on how to effectively make the general public engage and participate in this community movement. Our greatest idea was to make and publish this very website. As our generation is now digitally-inclined, we have arrived to a conclusion that a website would be the most effective medium of spreading awareness about water pollution. Though this site, we envision a wider audience and an easier form of reaching out to people.
